g′αruw, ‘to cut’, pret. jα:r m′ə, fut. g′α:r̥ə m′ə, past part. g′α:r̥ə, imperf. pass. jα:rti:, g′α:r̥αχə, plur. of g′αruw, ‘cutting pains’

α:r̥uw, ‘change’ infin. to M.Ir. aitherraigim
